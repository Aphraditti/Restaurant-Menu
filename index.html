About Us:

Authentic Thai food cuisine from the island of Phucket Thailand.

Origin Story:

As a little girl in Phucket Thailand, Mai Lu grew up in her parent’s restaurant and learned first hand how to prepare and cook delicious Thai dishes. Mai went on to attend the prestigious Karon cooking academy, where she sharpened her skills as an Executive chef. After graduation Mai traveled the world, but fell in love with the city of San Diego California and decided to stay and opened up the flagship restaurant H-Thai-ML. “I want to share my love and passion for authentic Thai cuisine with the world!” Mai has expressed and true to her words there is now a total of 4 H-Thai-ML Restaurants on the west coast and many more to come.
